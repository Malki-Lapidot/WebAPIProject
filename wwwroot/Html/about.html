About Us

Welcome to our Job Portal, a platform designed to connect job seekers and employers with ease and efficiency. Our website is tailored for both employers and job seekers, with different roles and permissions to enhance the user experience.

Our Roles and Permissions:

  • Manager:

    Managers have the ability to create, update, and delete their own job listings. They can also update their personal user profile information but do not have access to other users' data or job listings.

  • User:

    Users can browse job listings, apply for jobs, and view the details of each job. They can also update their personal profile information, but they cannot create, edit, or delete any job listings.

Our Mission:

We aim to provide an easy-to-use and secure environment for job seekers and employers alike. Whether you're looking for a new opportunity or seeking to fill a position, our platform is here to support you in every step of the way. With a simple interface, flexible role management, and comprehensive features, we strive to make job hunting and recruitment a seamless experience.

Thank you for using our platform, and we hope you find success in your career journey!
